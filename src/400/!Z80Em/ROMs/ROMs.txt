Z80Em: ROMs
===========

The following ROMs are supported:

48k                 The original ZX Spectrum 48k ROM

128k                The ZX Spectrum 128k ROM
+2                  The ZX Spectrum +2 ROM
+2Fr                The French ZX Spectrum +2 ROM

IMCEOR              Ian Collier's ROM (differences from standard ROM)
GrootEOR            Henk de Groot's ROM (differences from standard ROM)
HTREOR              roehrig@ergo-edv.de (differences from standard ROM)
TK95                TK 95 ROM (not supplied)

They should be placed in the directory '!Z80Em.ROMs'
